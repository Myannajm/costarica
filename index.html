PURA VIDA IN COSTA RICA

What if I moved to Costa Rica?

This was the question I asked myself back in 2022 that started it all. Fresh out of a breakup, and with the looming thought of graduation closing in on me, I desperately wanted to feel like my life would have some sort of adventure to it after I entered into the working world. In imagining what I wanted life to be like, I found myself gravitating to a no-stress life for a year abroad on the beaches of Costa Rica. And thus, Pura Vida with Myanna was born.

So what is Pura Vida with Myanna?

Pura Vida with Myanna is a lifestyle blog documenting my year living abroad in Costa Rica as an American. It contains my travel and accomodation plans, places I visited, tips and tricks I wish I had known, hidden cultural gems, and so much more! Pura Vida with Myanna is an accumulation of all the culture and experiences I had, in order to help you experience la pura vida de costa rica!
